BATTLE SUITS (v1.00)

2-player card battle game played using one standard 52 card deck

### BASIC RULES ###############################################################

- The objective is to have more cards in your Score Pile than your opponent by
  the time the Deck is empty.
- Both players begin with 12 cards, and populate their back row with 3 Units
  before play begins. One Unit is made of two cards. A game of RPS determines
  who goes first.
- All cards deployed to the field must always be faceup.
- The ace and face cards are valued as such: A = 1, J = 11, Q = 12, K = 13

### GAME LAYOUT ###############################################################

####...   P1 Hand       # P1 Score Pile
#A #2 #3  P1 Back Row
56 78 9J  P1 Front Row
#  #  #                 # Deck  # Discard Pile
56 78 9J  P2 Front Row
#A #2 #3  P2 Back Row
####...   P2 Hand       # P2 Score Pile

### SUITS BEHAVIOR ############################################################

HEART: Damage resistance; a HEART is immune to capture from a SPADE of lower
  value. For SPADE and HEART of equal value, the attacking Unit must sacrifice
  itself to capture this unit.

SPADE: Attack power; a SPADE can capture any Unit unless it has a HEART of
  greater value.

DIAMOND: Purchase power; a DIAMOND can purchase a shop card of equal or lesser
  value. This discards the DIAMOND card from the Unit and replaces it with the
  purchased card.

CLUB: Luck card; a CLUB of greater or equal value to a shop card may draw a card
  from the Deck when it is in the front row. This discards the CLUB card from
  the Unit and replaces it with the drawn card.

### ACTIONS ###################################################################

DEPLOY: When the player has two or more cards in their Hand, they may form a
  Unit and place it in a vacant spot in the back row.

MOVE: Move a Unit into an adjacent space. If that space is occupied, swap the
  position of those Units.

REFACTOR: When two adjacent Units are in the back row, swap one card between
  them. Or, when one Unit is in the back row and at least one card is in
  the player's Hand, swap one card between the player's Hand and that Unit.

ATTACK: When one Unit is in the front row and has a SPADE, capture the opponent
  Unit in the front row directly across from it and send both cards to the
  attacker's Score Pile.
    SPADE vs no HEART  The attacking Unit remains.
    SPADE = HEART      Both cards in the attacking Unit are discarded.
    SPADE < HEART      Attacking is not allowed.
    SPADE > HEART      The attacking Unit remains.
    DOUBLES: If both cards in the attacking Unit are necessary to defeat the
      target Unit (for instance, for [5 SPADE + 4 SPADE > 6 HEART], BOTH cards
	  in the attacking Unit are discarded.)

PURCHASE: When a Unit is in the front row and has a DIAMOND of greater or equal
  value to the card in the Shop Row, the DIAMOND is discarded and replaced with
  the card that was purchased. The vacancy in the Shop Row is immediately
  replaced with a card drawn from the Deck.
    DOUBLES PURCHASES: If both cards in the purchasing Unit are necessary to
      purchase the target card (for instance, [5 DIAMOND 4 DIAMOND > 6 SPADE],
	  BOTH cards in the purchasing Unit are discarded.)
	  
CLUB PURCHASE: Same as Purchase, except the player draws from the Deck instead
  of the Shop.

DRAW: When a player has 5 or fewer cards across both rows and their Hand, they
  may draw a card and add it to their Hand. If the Deck is empty, the game ends.
