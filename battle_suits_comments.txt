attacker's hand. This makes it easy for an attacking player to "hit and run"
leaving no opportunity for counterattack; what if capturing with one Spade left
the partner card as a "1 card unit" which was placed sideways? This unit could
act and move as a single card unit until captured or converted back into a 2
card unit via Refactor. Counterattacking players could capture that one
individual card.
